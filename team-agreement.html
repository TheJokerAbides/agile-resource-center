Purpose
⌄
To align our Agile team on shared principles, work norms, and collaboration practices while delivering secure, scalable, and high-quality solutions on AWS and Salesforce. This agreement promotes accountability, transparency, and continuous improvement.

Communicate transparently and treat each other respectfully.

Continuously reflect, learn, and improve together.

8
UAT Testing & Testing Best Practices
⌄
🔍 UAT testing setup
  • Environment: Salesforce Sandbox + AWS UAT environment
  • Scenarios: Defined with business, tested via Jira
  • Execution: End of sprint; business SME sign-off required
  • Bugs: Tracked in Jira with UAT label
🧪 Testing best practices
⚠️UAT, SIT or Stage testing should NOT be completed by a Developer unless the UAT team is unable to test by themselves and have requested assistance.
⚠️No one should be changing or deleting anything in UAT without agreement from the internal UAT team or a deployment, with an email notification.
ℹ️Test Capacity Tickets: Teams will add a 0-point ticket in sprints where UAT support is going to be needed.
ℹ️The internal UAT team will reach out to the people they need to help troubleshoot or ask questions about functionality.
ℹ️Test result issues derived from UAT/SIT/STAGE should be opened by the internal UAT team or business and sent to the Scrum Master to work with PO on prioritisation.
✓All issues and enhancements found should be logged in the issues list right away — even if discussion came from a different meeting. Anyone can open an issue item.
✓Tickets created in UAT that aren't accepted as backlog should be notated and allow the internal UAT team to cancel, so that they are aware.
✓Any questions on Jira tickets created from UAT outcomes and prioritised for the UAT timeframe should include the internal UAT team.
ℹ️UAT POC: The POC needs to be the internal UAT team and they should be involved until tickets move to a backlog item for a different release.
General testing approach
AWS testing
  • Shift-left: Early and automated testing
  • Unit, integration, and security tests
  • Checkov for infrastructure scanning
  • cfn-lint for CloudFormation linting
Salesforce testing
  • Apex tests for code coverage
  • UI walkthroughs and validation
  • Metadata checks before deployment
CI/CD pipeline (Jenkins)
Linting→ Unit tests→ Integration tests→ Deploy to QA/UAT
9
Agreement Maintenance
⌄
Review cadence: This agreement is reviewed twice a year, or when there is a major change in scope, team composition, or tooling. All team members are responsible for flagging when the agreement needs updating.
